ALGEBRA PLACEMENT

Test Grader · 25 Questions

A two-part system for grading 25-question multiple-choice placement tests: a printable bubble sheet for students, and a camera-based scanner that grades a stack of completed sheets in your browser.

STEP 1

Printable Sheet →

Print one per student. Has the corner markers the scanner needs.
STEP 2

Scanner →

Set the answer key, photograph completed sheets, grade in batch, export CSV.
Quick start:
  1. Open the Printable Sheet and print one copy per student.
  2. Students fill in answers with a #2 pencil or dark pen.
  3. Open the Scanner, set the answer key, then add photos.
  4. Hit "Process All", review any failed scans, export CSV.
